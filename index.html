About Me

My name is Ciaran Skelly, I am 18 years old. I live in Lusk, Co.Dublin. I attended Lusk Community College for my second level education. I am now studying Computer Applications at Dublin City University. I chose to do computer applications at DCU because I enjoyed learning how to build my own computer for playing games.
